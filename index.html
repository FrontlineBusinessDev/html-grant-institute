About the School

Rooted in San Pablo, built for the workforce

For over fifteen years, Grant Institute of Trade and Technology has welcomed students from Brgy. San Jose and the wider San Pablo community, offering an education that treats real skill and real readiness as the point. We combine Senior High School academics with technical-vocational training so every graduate leaves with more than a diploma — they leave with a direction.

Our philosophy is simple: learning by doing. Students train in working kitchens, service labs, and hands-on classrooms alongside instructors who have worked in the industries they teach.

Our Mission

To equip every student with practical, industry-relevant skills and the character to use them well.

Our Vision

To be Laguna's most trusted institute for technical-vocational and senior high education.
